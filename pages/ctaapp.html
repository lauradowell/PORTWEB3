Community Tech Aid App

← →

2023 - SwiftUI

The Community Tech Aid App was developed to provide accessible information and support for beneficiaries receiving computers from the charity. Its goal is to help users get started with their new devices, offering guidance, helpful links, and resources that assist in building computer literacy. The app is designed with the understanding that many users are more familiar with mobile devices, making it easier for them to transfer their skills to their newly acquired laptops. By offering a user-friendly platform, the app aims to empower individuals to take their first steps towards digital inclusion and improve their tech skills.
