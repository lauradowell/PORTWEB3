2024 - Whatsapp Chat Bot

Circularly.AI is an interactive AI-driven WhatsApp chatbot designed to promote sustainability by offering personalized guidance on recycling, eco-friendly alternatives, and upcycling ideas. By using WhatsApp, it makes it easier to integrate sustainable practices into every day life. This project provides a platform for users to learn and take action towards a more sustainable lifestyle. Circularly.AI encourages users to make eco-conscious decisions in a creative and approachable manner.
