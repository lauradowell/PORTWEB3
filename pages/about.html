Hi there! I’m a Creative Technologist with a focus on Human-Computer Interaction. I work at the intersection of AI, UX design, and creative technology, always looking for ways to make tech more human and accessible. My goal is to create tools that help people, especially children, better understand and engage with the world around them. After finishing my Bachelor of Science in Creative Computing, right now, I’m diving deeper into UX/UI design through my master’s studies. I believe technology should be empowering, educational, and help bring people closer together.

When I’m not geeking out, you’ll probably find me on a side-quest adventure—traveling with no solid plan, striking up random conversations with strangers, or trying something new (I recently tried skiing for the first time and loved it—though I did break a ski pole, all limbs are mostly intact!).

I stay connected to my Andalusian roots, whether it’s wearing my flamenco dresses and riding as an amazona during the fair, or donning a mantilla for Semana Santa with my mom. You might also catch me dancing like nobody’s watching, keeping up with the news, visiting art museums to chat about them with friends, or experimenting with makeup. And my eclectic music taste? It’s the soundtrack to my 360-degree lifestyle. Life’s too short not to live!
