January 18, 2025

Adapting Traditional Recipes for Modern Health

I recently created a storyboard for a recipe app designed for older adults. This is part of a broader project that involves interviews with five individuals aged 60 and above, along with a subsequent study to understand their needs and preferences. The app aims to offer traditional recipes with personalized filters, enabling users to adapt dishes to their dietary preferences and health conditions. In this particular storyboard, I introduce Maria, a 65-year-old user with kidney issues, who is searching for anti-inflammatory alternatives to her favorite recipes.

January 21, 2025

Jerez Flamenco Fashion Week

I recently went to the fitting for the Flamenco Catwalk in Jerez, a yearly event that sets the tone for the dresses and styles that will be worn in the Feria. Alongside Seville, Jerez is the center for flamenco, and it's been truly an honor to be a part of this process as a model. I was fitted for various dresses, one of which I'm particularly excited about but won't tell you about until the day comes. It was fun to see the seamstresses and designers iterate on their projects and decide in real-time. It's truly an honor to be able to connect with my culture and the fabulous people behind its fashions. I will be walking 14 shows, I can't wait!
