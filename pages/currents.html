January 18, 2025

Adapting Traditional Recipes for Modern Health

I recently created a storyboard for a recipe app designed for older adults. This is part of a broader project that involves interviews with five individuals aged 60 and above, along with a subsequent study to understand their needs and preferences. The app aims to offer traditional recipes with personalized filters, enabling users to adapt dishes to their dietary preferences and health conditions. In this particular storyboard, I introduce Maria, a 65-year-old user with kidney issues, who is searching for anti-inflammatory alternatives to her favorite recipes.
